Welcome to FiOS! Installing your Internet Router is easy.

Before you start, you need to make sure your service is ready to connect. You can do this two ways:

  • Click here to check out your service ready date: the date your service is up
  • Find the service ready date in the confirmation email your received when you placed your FiOS order.

If the service ready date is today or in the past, you can proceed right now. Let’s get started!
